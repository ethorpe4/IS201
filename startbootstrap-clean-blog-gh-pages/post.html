About Me

My Family

My family looks normal in the picture above but trust me there's more chaos than meets the eye.

The reason my family is so chaotic is because there are so many of us. Don't get me wrong - I love coming from a big a family - but growing up with four sisters and a brother was a lot.

My brother is the olderst, then I have two older sisters and two younger sisters. Being in the middle definetly comes with pros and cons, just like any other spot in the family. Overall, I like where I am.

My parents met in Colorado, and that's where I lived my entire life until I moved to Brazil to serve the community there for 18 months.

Living in Brazil really showed me how amazing my family is. Every day I spent away from them, I grew more grateful for the way I was raised and the love I experienced at home.

My Interests

I have many different hobbies ranging from music and movies to pretty much anything outdoors.

I have participated in many different music programs in the past and even though I don't participate in specific programs anymore it is still one of my all time favorite things to do in my free time. Throughout middle and high school I participated in choir and band. I play the flute. I also participated in musical theatre being in quite a few different shows.

Now that I don't have programs to participate in I love to sing and play my ukulele when I have free time.

I also love playing my ukulele around campfires and being outside. Being from colorado I grew up hiking and camping. I also enjoy paddleboarding and rock climbing. In the winter I also snowboard. I basically wouldn't exsite without the mountains

I love finding songs or movies that speak to me and combine many of my hobbies and passions. An artist that does this super well is Lizzy McAlpine. I especially love her song "To The Mountians" (you can listen to it below) I think it shows perfectly how the mountains can heal you.
